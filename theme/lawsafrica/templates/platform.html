Choose the Knowledge Base API when your product needs reliable answers, search results or AI grounding. Choose the Content API when you need full legislation collections inside your own systems.

Knowledge Base API

Query authoritative African legal information for AI grounding, RAG, legal agents, search and workflow tools. Laws.Africa handles ingestion, indexing and updates.

Pull full legislation collections into your own systems with Akoma Ntoso XML, HTML, PDF and metadata. Use it for internal databases, deep integration, compliance platforms and offline processing.

Frequently asked questions

How do upgrades work?

Start in Sandbox, move to Build when you need production access, and move to Scale for multiple countries or deeper integrations.

Can I change countries?

Sandbox and Build are country-scoped. Choose the country that matches your current product or evaluation need.

What happens when I hit limits?

Limits protect shared infrastructure. Upgrade when you need higher usage, production reliability or broader coverage.

What formats are supported?

The Content API supports structured legislation formats such as Akoma Ntoso XML and HTML, with PDF where available.

Do I need the Content API?

Use the Knowledge Base API for AI grounding, search and retrieval. Use the Content API when you need full local copies.

Can I use this with AI systems?

Yes. The platform is designed to ground AI and agent workflows in maintained, authoritative African legal knowledge.
