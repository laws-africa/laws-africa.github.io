      <h2>Legislation and Policy Document Digitisation</h2>

      <p class="lead">
        We are leaders in converting African legal and policy materials into consistent, machine-readable <a href="https://akomantoso.org">Akoma Ntoso XML (AKN)</a>,
        the industry standard format for legislative documents.
      </p>

      <div class="row mb-3">
        <div class="col-md-4">
          <div class="media mb-3">
            <div class="mr-3"><i class="fas fa-fw fa-flask fa-2x text-muted"></i></div>
            <div class="media-body">
              XML is <b>machine-readable</b> and enables access-to-justice innovations through analysis and machine learning technologies.
            </div>
          </div>
        </div>

        <div class="col-md-4">
          <div class="media mb-3">
            <div class="mr-3"><i class="fas fa-fw fa-file-pdf fa-2x text-muted"></i></div>
            <div class="media-body">
              XML enables a wide range of <b>automated publication outputs</b>, including for web, mobile, e-book (Kindle, EPUB) and print-ready PDF from a single source of truth.

          </div>
        </div>

        <div class="col-md-4">
