Bianca Braun is the Chief Financial Officer at Laws.Africa, where she leads financial strategy, operations, and governance across the organisation. With over 25 years of international experience, she specialises in integrating finance with business operations to support sustainable growth across both non-profit and commercial environments.

Her expertise includes financial governance and compliance (US GAAP, IFRS, SOX), funder and grant management, and the implementation of financial systems that improve efficiency and transparency. Bianca has worked extensively across Africa and Europe in senior finance roles, and as a fractional financial director to organisations in the social impact and technology sectors.
