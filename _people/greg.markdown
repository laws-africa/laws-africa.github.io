Greg is a co-founder of Laws.Africa, where he leads the development of legal information systems that provide free, structured access to legislation, case law, and government gazettes across multiple African countries. A software engineer by background, he focuses on building reliable, scalable systems and the pragmatic application of AI to improve how legal information is created, maintained, and accessed.

His work sits at the intersection of public infrastructure, legal systems, and sustainable business models for open access to law. Greg works closely with governments, legal professionals, and partner organisations across Africa. Before founding Laws.Africa, he worked at OpenUp and Amazon Web Services (AWS), and holds an MSc in Computer Science from the University of British Columbia.
